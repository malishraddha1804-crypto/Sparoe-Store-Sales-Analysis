# 🛍️ Sparoe Store Annual Sales Analysis 2022

## 📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes the annual sales data of Sparoe Store for the year 2022.
The goal is to find key insights about sales trends, customer behavior, 
and platform performance to help the business make better decisions.

---

## 🛠️ Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel (Pivot Tables, Charts, Data Cleaning)
- GitHub (Version Control & Project Hosting)

---

## 📊 Dataset Info
- Total Orders: 31,047
- Total Revenue: ₹2,11,76,377
- Time Period: January 2022 – December 2022
- Columns: 23 (Order ID, Gender, Age, Month, Status, Channel, Category, Amount, State, etc.)

---

## 🔍 Key Business Insights

### 1. 👩 Women Drive the Sales
- Women contributed **64% of total sales** (₹1,35,62,773)
- Men contributed **36% of total sales** (₹76,13,604)
- **Insight:** Marketing campaigns should target women more

### 2. 📅 March is the Best Month
- Highest sales in **March** (₹19,28,066 | 2,819 orders)
- Sales declined steadily after March
- **Insight:** Run promotions in Jan-Feb to boost early months

### 3. 📍 Maharashtra is the Top State
- Maharashtra leads with **₹29,90,221** in revenue
- Followed by Karnataka (₹26,46,358) and Uttar Pradesh (₹21,04,659)
- **Insight:** Focus inventory and delivery in these top 3 states

### 4. 🛒 Amazon is the Most Used Platform
- Amazon: **35.5%** of total orders
- Myntra: **23.4%**
- Flipkart: **21.6%**
- **Insight:** Prioritize Amazon listings and ads

### 5. ✅ Delivery Rate is Very High
- Delivered: **92.3%** (28,641 orders)
- Cancelled: 2.7% | Returned: 3.4% | Refunded: 1.6%
- **Insight:** Operations are efficient — very low cancellation rate

### 6. 👥 Adult Women are the Biggest Buyers
- Adult Women: **34.6%** of total orders
- Adult Men: **15.5%**
- Teenager Women: **21.1%**
- **Insight:** Target adult women aged 25-45 for best results

### 7. 📆 Tuesday has Highest Sales
- Tuesday: ₹37,62,642 (highest)
- Thursday: ₹22,07,368 (lowest)
- **Insight:** Run flash sales or ads on Tuesday

## ✅ Conclusion
- Target **women aged 25-45** for maximum sales impact
- Focus on **Maharashtra, Karnataka, UP** for regional campaigns
- Invest more in **Amazon** platform marketing
- Run promotions in **January-February** to improve slow months
- **Tuesday** is the best day to run flash sales or discount offers
